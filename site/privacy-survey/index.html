Privacy Policy & Survey Information

Last Updated: February 25, 2026

About This Survey

Thank you for taking the time to complete the PHLYXR survey. Your feedback is invaluable in helping us develop a product that truly meets the needs of our community.

What We're Asking

This survey takes approximately 5 minutes to complete and covers:

  • Your current sexual health practices
  • Features that matter most to you
  • Pricing preferences
  • Product packaging preferences
  • Demographic information (optional)

How We Use Your Responses

Your survey responses help us:

  • Refine the product formulation based on user preferences
  • Determine optimal pricing that balances accessibility and sustainability
  • Design packaging that meets user needs
  • Understand our target market better
  • Shape our clinical trial design around real-world use cases

Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

When you complete our survey or join our mailing list, we collect:

Survey Responses: - Answers to survey questions - Optional demographic information (age range, location) - Optional contact information (if you choose to provide it)

Mailing List: - Email address (required) - First name (optional) - Preferences for updates (optional)

Automatically Collected: - Form submission timestamp - Device type (mobile/desktop) - General location (country level only)

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

✅ Product Development - Shape PHLYXR based on user needs
✅ Communication - Send updates about development and launch
✅ Research - Analyze trends and preferences
✅ Clinical Trial Recruitment - Invite interested participants (opt-in only)
✅ Marketing - Understand our audience better

What We DON'T Do

❌ We do NOT sell your data to third parties
❌ We do NOT share identifiable information without consent
❌ We do NOT spam - you can unsubscribe anytime
❌ We do NOT use your data for unrelated purposes

Data Storage & Security

  • Storage: Your data is stored securely using Google Forms/Google Workspace with encryption
  • Access: Only core PHLYXR team members have access to survey data
  • Retention: We retain survey data for product development purposes. Mailing list data is kept until you unsubscribe
  • Anonymization: Survey responses are analyzed in aggregate and anonymized for research purposes

Your Rights (GDPR Compliant)

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Object to data processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Data portability - receive your data in a usable format

To exercise these rights: Email privacy@phlyxr.com

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses:

  • Essential cookies - For website functionality
  • Analytics cookies - Google Analytics (anonymized IP)
  • No advertising cookies - We don't use tracking for ads

You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

Children's Privacy

PHLYXR is intended for adults aged 18+. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18. If you believe we have collected data from a minor, please contact us immediately.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in:

  • United Kingdom (primary location)
  • European Union (if using EU-based services)
  • United States (Google services)

All transfers comply with GDPR requirements.

Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach:

  • We will notify affected individuals within 72 hours
  • We will report to relevant authorities (ICO in UK)
  • We will take immediate steps to secure data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of changes.

Survey-Specific Information

Anonymity

  • Survey responses are anonymous by default unless you choose to provide contact information
  • We cannot link anonymous responses to individuals
  • Aggregate data may be used in publications or presentations

Optional Contact Information

If you provide your email in the survey:

  • We may contact you about clinical trial opportunities (opt-in only)
  • You may receive early access offers for product launch
  • You can opt out of these communications at any time

Research Use

Anonymized survey data may be used for:

  • Academic publications
  • Investor presentations
  • Market research reports
  • Product development documentation

Your identifiable information will NEVER be included in public research.
