## What we are building {#what}

PHLYXR is a next generation lubricant concept designed to combine a premium feel with an added protective function.

<div class="feature-grid">

<div class="feature-card">
<div class="feature-icon">💧</div>
<div class="feature-title">Feels like a premium lubricant</div>
<p>Texture and sensation matter. We are designing PHLYXR to be enjoyable to use, not a compromise.</p>
</div>

<div class="feature-card">
<div class="feature-icon">🦠</div>
<div class="feature-title">Designed around the vaginal microbiome</div>
<p>pH aligned to the typical vaginal range and avoiding common irritants, so it is gentler on vaginal comfort.</p>
</div>

<div class="feature-card">
<div class="feature-icon">🛡️</div>
<div class="feature-title">A new layer of prevention</div>
<p>Our formulation approach aims to bind and neutralise pathogens whilst acting as a spermicidal too. This is being tested in vitro.</p>
</div>

</div>

## How it works

<div class="steps">
  <div class="step">
    <div class="step-icon">1</div>
    <div>
      <strong>Apply like any other lubricant.</strong><br>
      No new behaviour, no interruption.
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="step">
    <div class="step-icon">2</div>
    <div>
      <strong>Creates a protective layer.</strong><br>
      Designed to stay in place during sex.
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="step">
    <div class="step-icon">3</div>
    <div>
      <strong>Targets transmission in the fluid layer.</strong><br>
      Lab testing is focused on pathogen binding and viability reduction.
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
