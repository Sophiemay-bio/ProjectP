## What is the purpose of the survey?

The purpose of this market research survey is to determine whether there is demand for a contraceptive gel lubricant that can prevent STI spread (transmission) between sexually active individuals. The ingredients contained in the gel have been shown in research to bind and block the parts of viruses and bacteria that would give them entry into cells (causing infection), therefore preventing infection. The STIs it may prevent include chlamydia, gonorrhoea, and HIV. It has also shown effect against HPV and HSV.

The device also has the potential to act as a non-invasive and non-hormonal contraceptive, this is a secondary purpose. It would provide additional security and peace of mind in certain circumstances, such as condom failure.

For such a device to be developed, there must be demand for it and the market size for this device must be determined. This includes market demographics and whether potential users would be willing to try such a device.
