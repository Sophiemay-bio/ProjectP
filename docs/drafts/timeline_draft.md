## 🔬 Research & Development Phases

### **Phase 1: Discovery & Formulation** ✅ Complete

**Duration:** 9 months (Q1-Q3 2025)

**Activities:**

- Literature review and competitive analysis
- Ingredient selection and sourcing
- Initial formulation development
- Proof-of-concept testing
- Rheological characterization

**Key Learnings:**

- Identified optimal pH range for microbiome safety
- Validated pathogen binding mechanism in principle
- Confirmed user-acceptable texture and viscosity

---

### **Phase 2: Preclinical Validation** 🔄 In Progress

**Duration:** 6 months (Q4 2025 - Q2 2026)

**Activities:**

- In vitro testing against Chlamydia, Gonorrhoea, HSV, HPV
- Microbiome impact studies (lactobacillus cultures)
- Spermicidal efficacy validation
- Formula optimization based on results
- Stability and shelf-life testing

**Success Criteria:**

- ≥80% reduction in pathogen viability
- No disruption to lactobacillus cultures
- 100% sperm immobilization
- 24-month shelf stability

---
